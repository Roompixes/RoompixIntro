F.A.Q.

  • What is this?
    • This is a NFT (non-fungible token) project based on procedurally generated pixel art rooms. Project is based on the Fantom network.
    • Made from more than 400 assets in total there can be billions of combinations - of which only the best 8192 are procedurally generated.
  • Prices
    • Starting at 1 FTM the mint price will go up by 1 FTM every 1000 rooms.
  • Rares
    • About 0.8% of all rooms have rare filters which may totally change how the room looks. Also, more than 0.1% of rooms will be unique, meaning it contains themed assets and surroundings specific for that room.
  • Roadmap
    • The future of this project depends on its success. The more popularity it gets, the more features will be implemented. We have a lot of ideas that would break the cycle of just a collectable - like - trait viewer, room explorer, rarity checker, rare/unique announcement bots and even extending current NFT with gamified tools.
  • Selling/Buying
    • We are working on integration with artion.io and paintswap.
  • If you are facing specific issues or have questions do not hesitate to contact us on our social platforms.
